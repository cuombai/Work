About Us

WHO WE ARE

Blackberry Uniforms is the leading clothing manufacturer and distributor in Kisumu. We have been providing the best quality and services for the past 30 years, thus building a great rapport amongst many institutions and individuals in the Western region and across Kenya.

OUR MISSION

To provide the best quality products and services at the best rates in the market.

OUR PROMISE

When entrusted with the opportunity to serve you, we devote ourselves to ensuring that you;

  • Benefit from our professional team who will play their part in promoting your business
  • Benefit from the reliable warranties and guarantees we offer
  • Experience satisfactory on-call support from our team
  • Cut down on costs attributable to our very competitive and considerate pricing on our goods and services

OUR CORE VALUES

Our values are integral to the continuity of Blackberry Uniforms and, consequentially, create a deep sense of identity, belonging, and purpose in our people, products, and services. These values are:

  • Accountability & Transparency
  • Impartiality & Teamwork
  • Commitment & Hard Work
  • Integrity
  • Discipline
  • Loyalty
